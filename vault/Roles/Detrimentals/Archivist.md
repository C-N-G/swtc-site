## Ability
If you die, the [[Antagonist]] learns every [[player]]'s [[identity]] during the night.

## Examples
- The Archivist is dismissed, and dies. The following night, all Antagonists learn every player's identity.
- The Archivist dies during the night. All Antagonists learn every player's identity.
- The Archivist is malfunctioning. The Archivist is dismissed, and dies. The following night, nothing happens because the Archivist was malfunctioning when they died.
