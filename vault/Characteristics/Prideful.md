## Ability
You can't die during the night. If you are obsessed about being Prideful you lose this ability.

## Examples
- John is the Traitor. John chooses to kill the Prideful player during the night. The Prideful player does not die.
- The Prideful player is obsessed about being Prideful, they lose their ability. John is the Traitor. John chooses to kill the Prideful player during the night. The Prideful player dies.
- The Prideful player is malfunctioning. John is the Traitor. John chooses to kill the Prideful player during the night. The Prideful player dies.

## How to Play
- Try to bait your opponents into wasting their killing abilities on you.
- You can rest mostly safe, in the knowledge that you are less likely to die during the night than other players. However, your likeliness to survive will make you more likely to be assumed to be the Antagonist by the Loyalist team.

## How to Narrate
- As long as the Prideful player is not obsessed about being Prideful, they cannot die during the night. If the Prideful player is obsessed about being Prideful, ensure that they die as normal.
