> [!infobox]
>  “Those who have nothing to hide, have nothing to fear.”

## Effect
All votes are public.

## Examples
- All players can see the votes cast by all other players during and after nominations.

## How to Narrate
- Ensure all votes held are public, and that all players can see the votes cast by other players, during and after nominations.
