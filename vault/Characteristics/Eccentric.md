## Ability
Each night, you have the ability of a different [[characteristic]].

## Examples
- Night 1, the Eccentric player has the ability of the Chaste characteristic. Night 2, the Eccentric player has the ability of the Diligent characteristic.
- The Eccentric player is malfunctioning. They do not have the ability of any characteristic.

## How to Play
- If you did not wake in the night, and you're alive, you can presume that you had a passive characteristic ability.
- As you never know when your ability might change, or what it might change to, be mindful of long-term strategies utilizing your current characteristic ability.

## How to Narrate
- Each night, give the Eccentric player the ability of a different characteristic. It can be the same, or different from the last.
