>  “Every little detail matters.”

## Ability
Each night, you learn if you are [[malfunctioning]]. This ability cannot [[Malfunctioning|malfunction]].

## Examples
- On the first night, the Meticulous player is malfunctioning. They wake up and learn that they are malfunctioning.

## How to Play
- As you will know when you are malfunctioning or not, you can always know if your role's ability will function correctly.

## How to Narrate
- Each night, If the Meticulous player is malfunctioning, wake them up and tell them that they malfunctioning. Send them back to sleep.
